# Contact IntentixLab

**Collaborate on GridLookout, Perceptive Apps, and CPUX**

IntentixLab is developing a practical architecture for perceptive software: systems where intentions, pulses, design nodes, objects, and CPUX flows can be made explicit, inspectable, and repeatable.

This contact page is for people who want to collaborate, test, build, or discuss:

- **Open-source GridLookout** — platform-neutral visual placement and rendering
- **Perceptive Apps** — applications designed around Situational Reality and pulse-based interaction
- **CPUX / Intention Space implementation** — proprietary runtime, authoring, and orchestration work
- **Research and writing** — papers, position statements, demonstrations, and technical critique

---

## Why Contact IntentixLab?

The central idea behind Intention Space is that software should not only process data. It should make the *path of perception and resolution* visible.

In this view:

| Area | Collaboration Possibility |
|---|---|
| GridLookout | Contribute to rendering, layout, platform drivers, and visual pulse representation |
| Perceptive Apps | Build applications where user actions are guided by visible situational reality |
| CPUX | Explore implementation, licensing, runtime architecture, and design tooling |
| Research | Discuss perception as architecture, Situational Reality, and traceable AI systems |

This aligns with the architectural position that perception can be treated as propagation, reflection, and resolution in a system design. fileciteturn5file0
