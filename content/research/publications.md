## 2026

### Intentions as Communication Context Boundary

**Pronab Pal**  
IntentixLab, Melbourne, Australia  
Draft version 0.1, 19 June 2026

**DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.20761558](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20761558)

**Abstract.** This research note proposes that Intention should be treated as a first-class communication anchor in Perceptive App development. It argues that a Signal carries context at two levels: first through an Intention as communication boundary, and second through trivalent Pulses, while Response carries ordinary payload data.
