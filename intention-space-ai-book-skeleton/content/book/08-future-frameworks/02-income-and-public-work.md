# Public Work and Income Streams

The public site can support both research and future income streams.

## Public material

The following can remain openly readable:

- book chapters
- conceptual explanations
- beginner labs
- selected GridLookout examples
- educational demonstrations

## Commercial surfaces

Future income can come from:

- guided learning products
- GridLookout-based design tools
- perceptual investigation workbenches
- consulting around Intention Space architecture
- AI-assisted educational systems

## The key balance

The public material should build trust. The commercial systems should solve practical problems.

The book helps ordinary readers understand why the tools exist.
