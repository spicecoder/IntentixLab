# From Book to Framework

This book can grow in parallel with implementation.

The writing should not wait for the full framework to be finished. The book can document the path of discovery while the software matures.

## Parallel tracks

- Book: explains the human problem and conceptual path
- GridLookout: implements perceptual placement
- PCS: generalizes perceptual coordinates
- CPUX: orchestrates understanding and execution
- AI Design Nodes: assist without replacing perception

## Why this matters

A framework without a human story can become abstract. A story without implementation can remain inspirational only.

Intention Space needs both.
