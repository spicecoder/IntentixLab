# Perception Precedes Formalization

Formal symbols are powerful, but they are rarely the first way a human meets an idea.

A child sees roundness before learning circumference. A child sees balance before learning equations. A child sees sameness before learning congruence.

This does not weaken mathematics. It explains the human path into mathematics.

## The role of perception

Perception organizes:

- what matters
- what belongs together
- what is changing
- what remains stable
- what relation deserves attention

Mathematics often begins when such stability becomes nameable.

## Intention Space view

Intention Space proposes that a learning system should not only present information. It should help organize perception so that symbols arrive at the right time.
