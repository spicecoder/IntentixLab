# CPUX for Understanding

CPUX means Common Path of Understanding and Execution.

In ordinary software, execution is often treated as a chain of operations. In learning, execution must also include understanding.

A student does not merely move from step 1 to step 2. The student must form enough perception for step 2 to become meaningful.

## CPUX in a geometry rider

A rider-solving CPUX may involve:

1. observe the diagram
2. identify candidate objects
3. detect known relations
4. test relevant theorems
5. attempt a proof step
6. check whether the field became more coherent

This is not just a workflow. It is orchestration of perception.
