# Field Coherence

A Field is the current situational reality of a learner, system, or investigation.

It contains what is active, unresolved, relevant, and possible.

## Coherence

A Field becomes coherent when the relevant Pulses support each other enough for action to make sense.

In a proof, coherence may appear when:

- the right triangle is recognized
- an angle relation is seen
- a theorem becomes relevant
- the next step feels necessary

## Why coherence matters

Without coherence, the learner may act randomly. With coherence, the learner acts with direction.

This is why Intention Space treats learning as field evolution, not only content delivery.
