# AI as a Design Node

In Intention Space, AI should not be treated as the whole system.

AI is better treated as a Design Node: a bounded participant that receives signals and emits signals.

## Why this boundary matters

If AI is allowed to become the entire system, its output can appear authoritative even when the learner's perception has not stabilized.

A Design Node boundary keeps AI useful and accountable.

## AI can help with

- extracting candidate Pulses
- suggesting missing relations
- generating examples
- comparing learner attempts
- proposing next actions

But the Field still matters. The learner's situation still matters. The CPUX still matters.
