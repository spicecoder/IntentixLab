# AI and the Perception Gap

Modern AI can produce explanations, summaries, examples, and code. This is powerful.

But a learner may still fail to see the relation that matters.

This is the perception gap.

## Information is not enough

A student can receive ten explanations and remain confused. The problem may not be shortage of information. The problem may be that attention has not stabilized around the right relation.

## What Intention Space adds

Intention Space asks AI to participate in perceptual orchestration:

- identify learner pulses
- detect unresolved states
- place visual and conceptual elements meaningfully
- guide attention toward coherence
- let the learner act, not merely receive

The goal is not AI as answer machine. The goal is AI as perceptual support.
